Khinkali

Khinkali is a dumpling in Georgian cuisine. It is made of twisted knobs of dough, stuffed with meat, fish or vegetables and spices.

Ingredients

  • meat (lamb or beef and pork mixed)
  • onions
  • chili pepper
  • salt
  • cumin

Recipe instructions

  1. Add 1.1 kilo of flour to a mixing bowl. Make a depression in the middle of the flour and add the eggs.
  2. Add 450 ml of warm water.
  3. Mix the ingredients from the middle of the bowl until all of the flour is mixed.
  4. The dough should be formed into a ball.
  5. Roll out the dough until it is about 1/3 of an inch thick.
  6. Cut out circles of about 2.5 inches in diameter with a drinking glass.
  7. Add 1 heaped tbs of the meat mixture to the center of the round.
  8. Use your thumbs and index fingers to make an accordion type fold all around the outside.
  9. Carefully place the dumplings into a deep pan of boiling salty water, about 10-15 at a time.
