Khachapuri

Khachapuri is a traditional Georgian dish of cheese-filled bread.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • egg
  • oil

Recipe instructions

  1. Add the grated cheese, one egg and 20 grams of butter to a mixing bowl.
  2. Knead the dough before using. Leave for 10 minutes and then make a circular shape, with a lip around the edge.
  3. Spread the cheese filling onto the middle of the dough.
  4. Fold the edges of the dough over the cheese filling.
  5. A rolling pin can also be used to flatten the dough.
  6. Lightly dust a baking tray with flour (to stop the khachapuri sticking) and put the khachapuri in a pre-heated oven for about 15 minutes at a medium temperature.
